How to cook Okonomiyaki: The savory Japanese Pancake

Ingredients

Batter

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 5.6 oz nagaimo
  • 3/4 cup dashi
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup tenkasu
  • 1/4 cup pickled red ginger *optional*

Other

  • 1 head green cabbage
  • 1/2 lb sliced pork belly
  • neutral oil

Toppings

  • okonomiyaki sauce
  • japanese mayonnaise
  • katsuobushi *dried bonito flakes*
  • anori *dried seaweed*
  • green onions
  • pickled red ginger
